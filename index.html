Welcome to Waggy Tails

We are driven by our passion to provide the highest quality care and grooming for your fur babies. We cater to all breeds and all ages, and we have a team of professional and experienced groomers using only high-quality products and equipment to create a safe and stress-free environment.

We know that your fur babies are not just pets, they’re part of your family. You can be assured that your dog will get plenty of love and cuddles, and the odd treat (with your permission).

Our Range of Services

  • Oxygen-infused bath with shampoo and blow dry
  • Brushing and dematting
  • Haircut inc. pad shaving and sanitary trim
  • Nail trim
  • Ear cleaning, hair removal and flushing
  • Anal gland cleaning
