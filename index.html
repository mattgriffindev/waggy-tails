Welcome to Waggy Tails

We know that your fur babies are not just pets, they’re part of your family. We provide the highest quality of care in a safe and stress-free environment. You can be assured that your fur baby will get plenty of love and cuddles, and the occasional treat (with your permission).

Treatments & Prices

We offer a range of grooming services to ensure your fur baby can get the perfect groom for them

Long-haired breeds - Full Groom

Includes a pre-groom, two warm water baths, blow dry, full body styling, eyes and ears cleaned, and nail clip

Large: £65 / Small-Medium: £55 / Toy: £42

Short-haired breeds - Full Groom

Includes a pre-groom, two warm water baths, brush, blow dry, ears and eyes cleaned and nail clip. No body styling

Large: £40 / Small-Medium: £35 / Toy: £30

Puppy Groom (from 3 to 6 months)

A tailored groom just for puppies. Includes a warm water bath, brush through of the coat, gentle blow dry, ears and eyes cleaned, paw tidy (if needed) and nail clip

Large: £30 / Small-Medium: £25 / Toy: £25

Bath, Brush & Blow Dry

Perfect for in-between grooms. Includes a warm water bath, brush out of the coat and a blow dry - ideal for after muddy walks or trips to the beach

Large: £28 / Small-Medium: £25 / Toy: £22

Reasons to Choose Us

We like to think we stand out from the crowd; however, if you're not convinced, here are some reasons to choose us...

Highly Experienced

We have over 10 years' experience of providing high quality grooms.

Fully Qualified

Our groomers have achieved the Level 3 Diploma in Dog Grooming and Level 3 Award in Canine Emergency First Aid.

Natural Products

We use only vegan and cruelty free products to help prevent any allergic or chemical reactions.

Dog Welfare

We use a range of methods to create a stress-free salon, includng natural treats, tummy tickles, rests, and happy hoodies.

Modern Equipment

We use state of the art equipment that is reglarly and professionally cleaned and maintained.

Appointments to Suit All

We are open 6 days a week and we can book repeat grooms with text/email reminders.
